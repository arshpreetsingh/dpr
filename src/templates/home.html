What is a digital profile?

Profiling is the process of building a profile using information collected from available data. In general terms, a profile is a description of characteristics of a person or entity. A digital profile extends the latter term to include a digital representation, of a person or entity, which considers the characteristics and preferences of their online activity.
Digital footprints are a unique set of online activities, actions, and communications that leave a data trace on the Internet, computers, and other devices. It includes activities such as websites you visit, emails you send, and information you submit to online services.
